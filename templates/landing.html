Do I need design or CAD experience to use glymr?
No. glymr is designed for jewellery makers, not software engineers. You describe what you want, in words or by uploading a rough sketch, and the AI handles the technical execution. The 3D export and model try-on work the same way.
What file formats does the 3D CAD export support?
The CAD module exports GLB, OBJ, and STL. STL is the standard format for wax printers and most CNC casting setups. GLB is ideal for 3D viewers and presentations, and OBJ works with most professional CAD software for further refinement.
How accurate is the market pricing data?
Market data is pulled live via Google Search grounding at the time you run the analysis, so prices reflect current listings on platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, CaratLane, and BlueStone. The data is as fresh as the last time you refresh the query.
Can I use the generated images commercially?
Yes. All renders, model shots, and 3D exports you generate in glymr belong to you. You can use them for e-commerce listings, client pitches, catalogues, social media, and manufacturing without restrictions.
Does glymr work for all jewellery styles?
Yes, from Kundan and Polki bridal sets to contemporary minimalist fine jewellery, silver fashion jewellery, and everything in between. Just describe the style, material, and stones you want and the AI adapts accordingly.
